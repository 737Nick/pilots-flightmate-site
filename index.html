Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2025

Pilot's FlightMate does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device and does not require user registration or account creation.

Data Collection: We do not collect or transmit any personal data. Downloaded roster information and any calculations remain on your device.

Third-Party Services: This app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security: All data stays locally on your device. No information is transmitted externally.

Contact: If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can email us at support@flightm8.app.
